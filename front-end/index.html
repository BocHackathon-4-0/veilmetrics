About Tier 1
This tier aims to provide easy access to raw data sources and indicators spanning four core categories: technical, fundamental, market psychology, and on-chain. Aggregating all this information and making it widely available through a single user-friendly platform, reduces fragmentation and complexity for the crypto markets. The data will be downloadable and accessible through a simple API, at a price level that does not prohibit the participation of individuals and small teams.
